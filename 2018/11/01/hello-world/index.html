About me

Posted on 2018-11-01

Welcome to Feng Long(龙凤)’s homepage!

I am a graduate student at Kavli Institute for Astronomy and Astrophysics of Peking University, working with Prof. Gregory Herczeg.

I study the formation and evolution of protoplanetary disks orbiting around low-mass stars, primary using observations from the Atacama Large Millimeter/Submillimeter Array, to understand the initial conditions of planet formation.
